Passive Procrastination

<< Return to Home

Definition

Passive procrastination is the phenomenon of putting important tasks aside in favor of doing nothing of value. This is what most people conceive procrastination is, and is the most prominent type of procrastination.

Affects on Mental Health

Relation to Productivity

Examples

  • Taking naps instead of taking out the trash.
  • Playing video games instead of exercising.
  • Browsing social media instead of searching for apartments.
  • Binge-watching TV shows instead of interacting with friends.
